About The Project

What UJ FileHub does

UJ FileHub is a university platform designed to help students share educational resources in a more organized way. Instead of asking around for lecture slides or lab answers, students can use the website to upload and download helpful materials.

The main goal of the project is to make studying easier and more collaborative for students at the University of Jeddah. Through UJ FileHub, students can search for specific course documents, explore study guides, and ensure they have all the files they need to succeed.

Our website focuses on clean design, user-friendly navigation, and responsive pages so the platform works well on any device. The project combines front-end design with a database architecture to securely store and retrieve files for the student body.
