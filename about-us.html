About The Project

What TeamUp UJ does

TeamUp UJ is a university platform designed to help students build stronger project teams in a more organized way. Instead of searching randomly for teammates, students can use the website to present their skills, project interests, and preferred roles.

The main goal of the project is to make collaboration easier for students at the University of Jeddah. Through TeamUp UJ, students can submit team-building requests, explore opportunities, and connect with others who have matching interests and academic goals.

Our website also focuses on clean design, user-friendly navigation, and responsive pages so the platform works well on different screen sizes. The project combines front-end design, structured forms, validation, and database integration to create a practical and modern web solution.
